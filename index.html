Antonin Cheymol

Virtual Reality PhD Student

Hello! I am a 3rd year PhD student at Inria Rennes, Hybrid Team, studying Body-based Interactions in Virtual Reality (MR) under the supervision of Dr. Ferran Argelaguet, Dr. Jean-Marie Normand, Dr. Anatole Lecuyer and Dr. Rebecca Fribourg.

I graduated a Master in HCI from the University Paris-Saclay in 2020. I also visited different research labs where I conducted diverse VR research works at the University of Tokyo (2020-2021, Cyber Interface lab), Korea University (2021, Media lab), and the University of Chicago (2023, Human Computer Integration lab).

My main research interests lies in understanding, improving and leveraging perceptual factors at play in VR experiences. I am particularly interested in the opportunity offered by VR to alter one's appearance, in order to augment users' experience and interaction potentials.
